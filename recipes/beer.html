Silver Bullets

A delicious snack to enjoy with friends or by yourself

This recipe is great! You go to the store and buy beer.

Ingredients

  • Money
  • Transportation (the type will depend on distance to a store)
  • Cans

Steps

  1. Find your money
  2. Get to a store
  3. Find a beer at store
  4. Buy a beer
  5. ENJOY!
